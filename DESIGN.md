## Brand & Style

Traveloop is a premium travel planning platform that blends **high-performance utility** with **immersive storytelling**. The brand personality is adventurous, sophisticated, and intuitive.

The design system uses a **Dark Mode First** approach to emphasize high-quality travel photography and maps. It employs **Glassmorphism** to create depth and a "layered" feel, making the interface seem modern and high-end.

## Colors

- **Primary (Azure #00C9FF)**: Used for key actions, progress indicators, and primary branding. It represents the sky and sea.
- **Secondary (Purple #9D50BB)**: Used for luxury highlights, premium features, and creative elements.
- **Background (#0A0A0A)**: A deep midnight black to provide the ultimate contrast for imagery.

## Typography

- **Headlines (Space Grotesk)**: A geometric typeface that feels futuristic and bold.
- **Body (Inter)**: The gold standard for UI legibility.

## Components

- **Glass Cards**: semi-transparent backgrounds with `backdrop-filter: blur(10px)`.
- **Dynamic Buttons**: Gradient backgrounds with subtle scale animations on hover.
